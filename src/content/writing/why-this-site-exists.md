title: Why This Site Exists
date: 2026-07-06
source: original
tags: [meta, permanence]

Everything I've published so far lives on platforms I don't control — Instagram
captions, Facebook posts, YouTube vlogs, Reddit listings. Platforms come in and
out of style. Accounts get flagged, algorithms bury archives, companies die.

This domain is the fix. One location, one host, plain markdown under version
control. Anything I write anywhere else gets a permanent copy here, with a link
back to where it originally ran. The platforms become distribution; this is the
source of truth.

Stack over sprint: this is a capability layer, not a redesign project. It starts
ugly and complete rather than pretty and empty.
